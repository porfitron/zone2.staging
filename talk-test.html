Quick mic check (optional)

At the effort you are curious about, read the line below aloud once. We analyze the recording only in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.

Say: I could keep this pace and hold a conversation.

This is a rough acoustic cue (pauses, uneven loudness, noisy speech). It is not medical advice and it is not a substitute for heart rate, RPE, or how you feel.

Common questions

Is the talk test a replacement for a heart rate monitor?

No. It is a practical field check you can use anywhere. A chest strap or trusted HR source is still the best way to hold a specific bpm band once you know your range.

What does the mic check actually measure?

We look for rough patterns in your clip, like long silent gaps and uneven loudness. That is not the same as measuring oxygen or lactate. Treat it as a nudge, not a lab test.

Does my recording leave my phone or computer?

No upload. Analysis runs in the browser. If you are offline-capable through a service worker, the same rule applies: audio stays on your device.
